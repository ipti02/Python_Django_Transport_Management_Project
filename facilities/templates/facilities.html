IIUC Transport Facilities

Bus Routes and Schedules

Provide detailed information about the routes the bus takes, including pick-up and drop-off points. Include the timings for each stop along the route.

Real-Time Tracking

Integrate a GPS system to allow students to track the location of the bus in real-time. This feature helps students plan their trips more efficiently.

Alerts and Notifications

Implement a notification system to inform students about delays, changes in schedule, or other important updates.

Booking and Reservations

Allow students to reserve seats in advance, especially during peak times. Provide options for one-time bookings and recurring reservations (e.g., for daily commuters).

Accessibility Information

Include details about wheelchair accessibility and any other facilities for students with special needs.

Fare Information

Display clear information about ticket prices, payment methods, and any available discounts (e.g., for semester pass holders).

Contact and Support

Provide contact information for the transport department or relevant personnel for inquiries, feedback, or reporting issues.

Safety Guidelines

Share important safety information for passengers, such as emergency procedures and rules for behavior on the bus.

Feedback and Reviews

Allow students to leave feedback about their experiences, which can help improve the service.

FAQ Section

Address common questions or concerns that students may have regarding the transportation system.

Integration with University Systems

If possible, integrate the transport system with the university's official app or website for seamless access.

Weather Updates

Provide weather forecasts or alerts that may impact the bus service, helping students plan accordingly.

Social Media Integration

Include links to social media pages or groups related to the transport service for updates and community discussions.

Emergency Contacts:

List emergency phone numbers for quick access in case of accidents, medical emergencies, or other critical situations.

User-Friendly Interface

Ensure that the website is easy to navigate and mobile-friendly for students accessing it on different devices.
